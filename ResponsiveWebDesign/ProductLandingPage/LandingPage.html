Maintaining your knife

To ensure the cutting power of your knives are maintained for a long time you must follow some basic care instructions, for example:

  • Always use it in a plastic or wooden cutting board, never use in on top of glass, stone or other hard surfaces, as it damages your knife;
  • You should use a honing rod frequently, whenerver you feel that your knife is not cutting like it used to. Pass it a couple of times on the rod a couple of times.
  • If after you honed it you still need to aply pressure in order make the cut it's time to sharpen your knife. We recommend using a sharpening stone or whetstone for that.
