Potatoes

From delicious snacks to alcoholic drinks

  • It's estimated that potatoes have been cultivated for around 8,000 years.
  • Wild species can be found along the Americas from Chile to Canada.
  • Potatoes were first domesticated by the Native Americans and were specially important in the Andes.
  • In the Inca Civilization potatoes were considered the main source of energy.
  • They were first taken to Europe by the Spanish after the invasion of the American continent.
  • Now adays potatoes are used in a miriad of dishes by several populations throughout the globe
  • Potatoes are used to make alcoholic beverages such as Vodka.
