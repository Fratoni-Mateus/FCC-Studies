HTML projects portfolio

In this page you will find a collection of my HTML and CSS projects as well as my personal studies regarding the topic.

Tribute page;

On this page the goal is to have a simple page to pay tribute. The topic I choose is a vegetable which is normally taken for granted, but has great global importance in various cultures: Potatoes.

Survey form;

Survey forms are the most simple and direct form of gathering information from a group of people. Surveys can be created for various purposes, and in this one the intention is to have some knowledge regarding food preferences and requests for an imaginary catering service.

Product Landing Page.

The internet is also a place for commerce, the first virtual purchase is open to discussion, but it's assumed to have been made on 1994 by Dan Kohn, where he bought a Sting's CD from a friend. This project was created to showcase a possible landing page of a knives manufacturer.
